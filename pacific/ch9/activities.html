Activities at Pacific Trials

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trials and is adjacent to a state park. Go to it alone or join one of our guidedd hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at the Pacific Trials Resort, we offer guided bird-watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
